Presidential Suite

The Penthouse Suite at Hotel Eden combines modern elegance with touches of architectural detail throughout and beautiful hardwood flooring. The furniture has been carefully selected to blend harmoniously with an array of decorative objects. From the suite’s rooftop location, there are magnificent views of Rome, courtesy of floor-to-ceiling windows. The suite features a study area, spacious living room for entertaining, and a dining area for up to eight people. The luxurious master bedroom connects to the master bathroom, which features exquisite mosaics, opulent marble vanities, luxurious Italian Bottega Veneta toiletries, a walk-in rain head shower, and a whirlpool bath with spectacular views of Rome and the private gardens of Villa Aurora.

Prestige Room

Reminiscent of an elegant Roman residence, these sophisticated and elegant Prestige Rooms with view at Hotel Eden are decorated with timeless Italian furnishings. They feature city views and are bright with soaring ceilings and tall windows, which have been designed to make the most of the natural daylight. Enjoy fine fabrics, beautiful artwork and exquisite design. Modern luxuries include master controlled lighting and climate control, Wi-Fi, Bang and Olufsen televisions and sound systems with Bluetooth connectivity. Delight in the stylish white and gold themed bathrooms, featuring exquisite mosaics, marble vanities, walk-in rain head showers, a separate bath, and Italian Bottega Veneta toiletries.
